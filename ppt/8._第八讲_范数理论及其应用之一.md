第八讲 范数理论及其应用

# 信息与通信工程学院 吕旌阳

本讲主要内容

定义: 如果 $\mathrm{V}$ 是数域 $\mathrm{K}$ 上的线性空间,且对于 $\mathrm{V}$ 的任一向量 $\mathrm{x}$ ,对应一个实数值 $\parallel \mathrm{x}\parallel$ ,满足以下三个条件

1) 非负性: $\parallel x\parallel  \geq  0$ ,且 $\parallel x\parallel  = 0 \Leftrightarrow  x = 0$

2) 齐次性: $\parallel \mathrm{k}\mathrm{x}\parallel  = \left| \mathrm{k}\right|  \cdot  \parallel \mathrm{x}\parallel ,\;\forall \mathrm{k} \in  \mathrm{K}$

3) 三角不等式: $\parallel x + y\parallel  \leq  \parallel x\parallel  + \parallel y\parallel$ 则称 $\parallel \mathrm{x}\parallel$ 为 $\mathrm{V}$ 上向量 $\mathrm{x}$ 的范数，简称为向量范数。

注意:2)中 $\left| k\right|$ 当K 为实数时为绝对值，

当K 为复数域时为复数的模。

向量的范数具有下列简单性质:

(1)当 $\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} \neq  0$ 时, $\begin{Vmatrix}{\frac{1}{\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix}}x}\end{Vmatrix} = 1 \; \because \begin{Vmatrix}{\frac{1}{\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix}}x}\end{Vmatrix} = \frac{1}{\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix}}\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} = 1$

(2) $\forall x \in  V\;,\;\begin{Vmatrix}{-x}\end{Vmatrix} = \begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} \; \begin{matrix} \because & \begin{Vmatrix}{-x}\end{Vmatrix} = \left| {-1}\right| \begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} = \begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} \end{matrix}$

(3) $\forall x, y \in  V,\parallel x\parallel  - \parallel y\parallel  \leq  \parallel x - y\parallel$

$\because \begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} = \begin{Vmatrix}{\left( {x - y}\right)  + y}\end{Vmatrix} \leq  \begin{Vmatrix}{x - y}\end{Vmatrix} + \begin{Vmatrix}y\end{Vmatrix} \Rightarrow  \begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} - \begin{Vmatrix}y\end{Vmatrix} \leq  \begin{Vmatrix}{x - y}\end{Vmatrix}$

(4) $\forall x, y \in  V,\begin{Vmatrix}{\left| x\right|  - \begin{Vmatrix}y\end{Vmatrix}}\end{Vmatrix} \leq  \begin{Vmatrix}{x - y}\end{Vmatrix}$

$\begin{Vmatrix}x\end{Vmatrix} = \begin{Vmatrix}{\left( {x - y}\right)  + y}\end{Vmatrix} \leq  \parallel x - y\parallel  + \parallel y\parallel  \Rightarrow  \parallel x\parallel  - \parallel y\parallel  \leq  \parallel x - y\parallel$

同样 $\begin{Vmatrix}\mathbf{y}\end{Vmatrix} - \parallel \mathbf{x}\parallel  \leq  \parallel \mathbf{y} - \mathbf{x}\parallel  = \parallel \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y}\parallel$

例 1: 线性空间 ${C}^{n}$ ,设 $x = {\left( {x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}\right) }^{T} \in  {C}^{n}$

1: $\parallel \mathrm{x}{\parallel }_{1} = \sum \left| {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{i}}\right|$ 是一种向量范数,记为1-范数

是一种向量范数,记为 $p$ -范数或 ${1}_{p}$ 范数

证明: 向量p - 范数 $\parallel x{\parallel }_{p} = {\left( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left| {x}_{i}\right| }^{p}\right) }^{\frac{1}{p}}\;\left( {1 \leq  p < \infty }\right)$

证: 性质 (1) 、 (2) 显然是满足的

设 $x = \left( {{\xi }_{1},{\xi }_{2},\cdots ,{\xi }_{n}}\right) , y = \left( {{\eta }_{1},{\eta }_{2},\cdots ,{\eta }_{n}}\right)$ ,则

$$
p = 1 : {\begin{Vmatrix}\mathbf{x} + \mathbf{y}\end{Vmatrix}}_{1} = \sum \left| {{\xi }_{\mathbf{i}} + {\eta }_{\mathbf{i}}}\right|  \leq  \sum \left| {\xi }_{\mathbf{i}}\right|  + \left| {\eta }_{\mathbf{i}}\right|  = {\begin{Vmatrix}\mathbf{x}\end{Vmatrix}}_{1} + {\begin{Vmatrix}\mathbf{y}\end{Vmatrix}}_{1}
$$

$\mathrm{p} > 1 : \mathrm{x} + \mathrm{y} = \mathbf{\theta }$ 时,结论成立; $\mathrm{x} + \mathrm{y} \neq  \mathbf{\theta }$ 时,应用Holder不等式

$$
\sum \left| {{a}_{i}{b}_{i}}\right|  \leq  {\left( \sum {\left| {a}_{i}\right| }^{p}\right) }^{\frac{1}{p}}{\left( \sum {\left| {b}_{i}\right| }^{q}\right) }^{\frac{1}{q}}\left( {p > 1, q > 1,\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} = 1}\right)
$$

(利用 $\frac{1}{\mathrm{q}} = 1 - \frac{1}{\mathrm{p}} = \frac{\mathrm{p} - 1}{\mathrm{p}} \Rightarrow  \left( {\mathrm{p} - 1}\right) \mathrm{q} = \mathrm{p}$ )

${\left( \parallel \mathrm{x} + \mathrm{y}{\parallel }_{\mathrm{p}}\right) }^{\mathrm{p}} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{\mathrm{i} = 1}}^{\mathrm{n}}{\left| {\xi }_{\mathrm{i}} + {\mathbf{\eta }}_{\mathrm{i}}\right| }^{\mathrm{p}} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{\mathrm{i} = 1}}^{\mathrm{n}}\left( {\left| {{\xi }_{\mathrm{i}} + {\mathbf{\eta }}_{\mathrm{i}}}\right|  \cdot  {\left| {\xi }_{\mathrm{i}} + {\mathbf{\eta }}_{\mathrm{i}}\right| }^{\mathrm{p} - 1}}\right) \; \leq  \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left| {\xi }_{i}\right| {\left| {\xi }_{i} + {\eta }_{i}\right| }^{p - 1} + \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left| {\eta }_{i}\right| {\left| {\xi }_{i} + {\eta }_{i}\right| }^{p - 1} \; \leq  {\left( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left| {\xi }_{i}\right| }^{p}\right) }^{1/p}{\left( \sum {\left( {\left| {\xi }_{i} + {\eta }_{i}\right| }^{p - 1}\right) }^{q}\right) }^{1/q} + {\left( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left| {\eta }_{i}\right| }^{p}\right) }^{1/p}{\left( \sum {\left( {\left| {\xi }_{i} + {\eta }_{i}\right| }^{p - 1}\right) }^{q}\right) }^{1/q} \; = \parallel \mathrm{x}{\parallel }_{\mathrm{p}}{\left( \sum {\left| {\xi }_{\mathrm{i}} + {\eta }_{\mathrm{i}}\right| }^{\mathrm{p}}\right) }^{1/\mathrm{q}} + \parallel \mathrm{y}{\parallel }_{\mathrm{p}}{\left( \sum {\left| {\xi }_{\mathrm{i}} + {\eta }_{\mathrm{i}}\right| }^{\mathrm{p}}\right) }^{1/\mathrm{q}}$

$$
\frac{1}{\mathrm{q}} = \frac{\mathrm{p} - 1}{\mathrm{p}}
$$

$= \left( {\parallel x{\parallel }_{p} + \parallel y{\parallel }_{p}}\right) {\left( \parallel x + y{\parallel }_{p}\right) }^{p - 1}$

因此: $\;\left( {\parallel x{\parallel }_{p} + \parallel y{\parallel }_{p}}\right)  \geq  \parallel x + y{\parallel }_{p}$

所以 $\parallel x{\parallel }_{p} = {\left( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{\left| {x}_{i}\right| }^{p}\right) }^{\frac{1}{p}}\;\left( {1 \leq  p < \infty }\right)$ 是向量 $x$ 的范数
