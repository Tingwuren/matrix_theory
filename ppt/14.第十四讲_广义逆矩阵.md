## 投影变换

定义:向量空间 ${C}^{n}$ 中，子空间 $L$ 与 $M$ 满足 ${C}^{n} = L \oplus  M$ ,对 $\forall x \in  {C}^{n}$ ,分解式

$x = y + z, y \in  L, z \in  M$ 唯一。称变换 ${T}_{L, M}\left( x\right)  = y$ 为沿着 $M$ 到 $L$ 的投影性质 (1): ${T}_{L, M}$ 是线性变换

性质 (2): $R\left( {T}_{L, M}\right)  = L, N\left( {T}_{L, M}\right)  = M$

性质 (3): $\;\forall x \in  L \Rightarrow  {T}_{L, M}\left( x\right)  = x\;\forall x \in  M \Rightarrow  {T}_{L, M}\left( x\right)  = \theta$

[注] ${\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}$ 是 $\mathrm{L}$ 中的单位变换

${\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}$ 是 $\mathrm{M}$ 中的零变换

## 二、投影矩阵

定义:取线性空间 ${C}^{n}$ 的基为 ${e}_{1},{e}_{2},\ldots ,{e}_{n}$ 时,元素 $x$ 与它的坐标形式一致。 称 ${T}_{L, M}$ 在该基下的矩阵为投影矩阵,记为 ${P}_{L, M}$

性质 (4): ${T}_{L, M}\left( x\right)  = y \Leftrightarrow  {P}_{L, M}x = y$

$$
\mathrm{X} \in  \mathrm{L} \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\left( \mathrm{X}\right)  = \mathrm{X} \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{X} = \mathrm{X}
$$

$$
\mathrm{X} \in  \mathrm{M} \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\left( \mathrm{x}\right)  = \theta  \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{x} = \theta
$$

预备: $R\left( A\right)  = \left\{  {y \mid  y = {Ax}, x \in  {C}^{n}}\right\}  , N\left( A\right)  = \left\{  {x \mid  {Ax} = 0, x \in  {C}^{n}}\right\}$

引理1: ${A}_{n \times  n},{A}^{2} = A \Rightarrow  N\left( A\right)  = R\left( {I - A}\right)$

证明: $\;{A}^{2} = A \Rightarrow  A\left( {I - A}\right)  = O$

先证 $R\left( {I - A}\right)  \subset  N\left( A\right)$

$$
\forall x \in  R\left( {I - A}\right)  \Rightarrow  \exists u \in  {C}^{n},{st}.x = \left( {I - A}\right) u
$$

$$
{Ax} = A\left( {I - A}\right) u = \theta  \Rightarrow  x \in  N\left( A\right)
$$

再证 $\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{A}\right)  \subset  \mathrm{R}\left( {\mathrm{I} - \mathrm{A}}\right)  : \;\forall \alpha  \in  \mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{A}\right)  \Rightarrow  \mathrm{A}\alpha  = \theta$

$$
\alpha  = \alpha  - \mathrm{A}\alpha  = \left( {\mathrm{I} - \mathrm{A}}\right) \alpha  \in  \mathrm{R}\left( {\mathrm{I} - \mathrm{A}}\right)
$$

故 $\;\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{A}\right)  = \mathrm{R}\left( {\mathrm{I} - \mathrm{A}}\right)$

定理1: $\;{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{n}} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}} \Leftrightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}^{2} = \mathrm{P}$

证明: 必要性 ${\mathrm{C}}^{\mathrm{n}} = \mathrm{L} \oplus  \mathrm{M}$

$$
\forall x \in  {C}^{n}, x = y + z, y \in  L, z \in  M\text{ 唯一 } \Rightarrow  {P}_{L, M}x = y
$$

$$
{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}^{2}\mathrm{x} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\left( {{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{x}}\right)  = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{y} = \mathrm{y} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{x}
$$

充分性 $\;\mathrm{\forall x \in  {C}^{n} \Rightarrow  x = {Px} + \left( {I - P}\right) x}$

令 $y = {Px} \in  R\left( P\right) , z = \left( {I - P}\right) x \in  R\left( {I - P}\right)  = N\left( P\right)$

则 ${C}^{n} = R\left( P\right)  + N\left( P\right)$ ,下证 $R\left( P\right)  \cap  N\left( P\right)  = \{ \theta \}$

对 $\forall \beta  \in  R\left( P\right)  \cap  N\left( P\right) \;\beta  \in  R\left( P\right)  \Rightarrow  \exists u \in  {C}^{n},$ st. $\beta  = {Pu}$

$$
\beta  \in  \mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{P}\right)  \Rightarrow  \mathrm{P}\beta  = \theta
$$

故 $\beta  = \mathrm{{Pu}} = {\mathrm{P}}^{2}\mathrm{u} = \mathrm{{PPu}} = \mathrm{P}\beta  = \theta$

于是可得 ${\mathrm{C}}^{\mathrm{n}} = \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{P}\right)  \oplus  \mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{P}\right)$ ,从而有

$$
x = y + z, y = {Px} \in  R\left( P\right) , z = \left( {I - P}\right) x \in  N\left( P\right) \text{ 唯一 }
$$

因为投影变换 ${\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{P}\right) ,\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{P}\right) }$ 满足

$$
{T}_{R\left( P\right) , N\left( P\right) }\left( x\right)  = y \Rightarrow  {P}_{R\left( P\right) , N\left( P\right) }x = y\;\left( {\forall x \in  {C}^{n}}\right)
$$

所以 $P = {P}_{R\left( P\right) , N\left( P\right) }$

## 三、投影矩阵的确定方法

$\dim L = r, L$ 的基为 ${x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{r} : X = \left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{r}}\right)$

$\dim M = n - r, M$ 的基为 ${y}_{1},\cdots ,{y}_{n - r} : Y = \left( {{y}_{1},\cdots ,{y}_{n - r}}\right)$

$$
\left. \begin{array}{l} {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{i}} = {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{i}}\; \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{X} = \mathrm{X} \\  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{j}} = \theta \; \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\mathrm{Y} = \mathrm{O} \end{array}\right\}   \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}\left( {\mathrm{X} \mid  \mathrm{Y}}\right)  = \left( {\mathrm{X} \mid  \mathrm{O}}\right)
$$

$$
\Rightarrow  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}} = \left( {\mathrm{X} \mid  \mathrm{O}}\right) {\left( \mathrm{X} \mid  \mathrm{Y}\right) }^{-1}
$$

例 1: ${\mathrm{R}}^{2}$ 中: ${\mathbf{\alpha }}_{1} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 0 \\  1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,{\mathbf{\alpha }}_{2} = \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} 1 \\   - 1 \end{matrix}\right\rbrack  ,\mathrm{L} = \mathrm{L}\left( {\mathbf{\alpha }}_{1}\right) ,\mathrm{M} = \mathrm{L}\left( {\mathbf{\alpha }}_{2}\right) ,\;$ 求 ${\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}$

解: ${P}_{L, M} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 0 & 0 \\  1 & 0 \end{array}\right\rbrack  {\left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} 0 & 1 \\  1 &  - 1 \end{matrix}\right\rbrack  }^{-1} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 0 & 0 \\  1 & 1 \end{array}\right\rbrack$

验证: $\;{P}_{L, M}{\alpha }_{1} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 0 & 0 \\  1 & 1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 0 \\  1 \end{array}\right\rbrack   = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 0 \\  1 \end{array}\right\rbrack$

$$
{\mathbf{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}{\mathbf{\alpha }}_{2} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 0 & 0 \\  1 & 1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} 1 \\   - 1 \end{matrix}\right\rbrack   = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 0 \\  0 \end{array}\right\rbrack
$$

例2: ${\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L},\mathrm{M}}$ 与 $\mathrm{L}$ 和 $\mathrm{M}$ 的基的选择无关。

证: $L$ 的基 ${x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{r}$ ; 另一基 ${\widetilde{x}}_{1},\cdots ,{\widetilde{x}}_{r}$

$$
X = \left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{r}}\right) ,\widetilde{X} = \left( {{\widetilde{x}}_{1},\cdots ,{\widetilde{x}}_{r}}\right)  \Rightarrow  \widetilde{X} = X{C}_{r \times  r}
$$

$M$ 的基 ${y}_{1},\cdots ,{y}_{n - r}$ ; 另一基 ${\widetilde{y}}_{1},\cdots ,{\widetilde{y}}_{n - r}$ :

$$
Y = \left( {{y}_{1},\cdots ,{y}_{n - r}}\right) ,\widetilde{Y} = \left( {{\widetilde{y}}_{1},\cdots ,{\widetilde{y}}_{n - r}}\right)  \Rightarrow  \widetilde{Y} = Y{D}_{\left( {n - r}\right)  \times  \left( {n - r}\right) }
$$

$$
\left( {\widetilde{X} \mid  \mathbf{O}}\right)  \cdot  {\left( \widetilde{X} \mid  \widetilde{Y}\right) }^{-1} = \left( {{XC} \mid  \mathbf{O}}\right)  \cdot  {\left\lbrack  \left( X \mid  Y\right) \left( \begin{array}{ll} \mathbf{C} & \mathbf{O} \\  \mathbf{O} & \mathbf{D} \end{array}\right) \right\rbrack  }^{-1}
$$

$$
= \left( {{XC} \mid  O}\right)  \cdot  \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} {C}^{-1} & O \\  O & {D}^{-1} \end{matrix}\right\rbrack   \cdot  {\left( X \mid  Y\right) }^{-1} = \left( {X \mid  O}\right)  \cdot  {\left( X \mid  Y\right) }^{-1}
$$

## 四、正交投影变换

定义:欧氏空间 ${\mathrm{C}}^{\mathrm{n}}$ 中，子空间 $\mathrm{L}$ 给定，取 $\mathbf{M} = {\mathbf{L}}^{ \bot  }$ ，则 ${\mathbf{C}}^{n} = \mathbf{L} \oplus  \mathbf{M}$ 正交投影变换 ${T}_{L} \triangleq  {T}_{L, M};\;$ 正交投影矩阵 ${P}_{L} \triangleq  {P}_{L, M}$

定理2: 方阵 $P = {P}_{L} \Leftrightarrow  {P}^{2} = P,{P}^{H} = P$

证明: 必要性: 由 $P = {P}_{L} \Rightarrow  {P}^{2} = P$

$$
\forall {x}_{1} \in  {C}^{n},{x}_{1} = {y}_{1} + {z}_{1},{y}_{1} \in  L,{z}_{1} \in  M
$$

$$
\forall {x}_{2} \in  {C}^{n},{x}_{2} = {y}_{2} + {z}_{2},{y}_{2} \in  L,{z}_{2} \in  M
$$

$$
{P}_{L}{x}_{1} = {y}_{1} \in  L,\left( {I - {P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{1} = {x}_{1} - {y}_{1} = {z}_{1} \in  {L}^{ \bot  }
$$

$$
{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} = {\mathrm{y}}_{2} \in  \mathrm{L},\left( {\mathrm{I} - {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}}\right) {\mathrm{x}}_{2} = {\mathrm{x}}_{2} - {\mathrm{y}}_{2} = {\mathrm{z}}_{2} \in  {\mathrm{L}}^{ \bot  }
$$

定理 2: 方阵 $\mathrm{P} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}$

$$
{P}_{L}{x}_{1} = {y}_{1} \in  L,\left( {I - {P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{1} = {x}_{1} - {y}_{1} = {z}_{1} \in  {L}^{ \bot  }
$$

$$
{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}{\mathrm{X}}_{2} = {\mathrm{y}}_{2} \in  \mathrm{L},\left( {\mathrm{I} - {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}}\right) {\mathrm{x}}_{2} = {\mathrm{x}}_{2} - {\mathrm{y}}_{2} = {\mathrm{z}}_{2} \in  {\mathrm{L}}^{ \bot  }
$$

$$
{P}_{L}{x}_{1} \bot  \left( {I - {P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{2} \Rightarrow  {x}_{1}^{H}{P}_{L}^{H}\left( {I - {P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{2} = 0 \Rightarrow  {x}_{1}^{H}\left( {{P}_{L}^{H} - {P}_{L}^{H}{P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{2} = 0
$$

$$
\left( {\mathrm{I} - {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}}\right) {\mathrm{x}}_{1} \bot  {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{x}}_{1}^{\mathrm{H}}{\left( \mathrm{I} - {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}\right) }^{\mathrm{H}}{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} = 0\; \Rightarrow  {\mathrm{x}}_{1}^{\mathrm{H}}\left( {{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}} - {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}^{\mathrm{H}}{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}}\right) {\mathrm{x}}_{2} = 0
$$

因此 ${X}_{1}^{H}\left( {{P}_{L}^{H} - {P}_{L}}\right) {x}_{2} = 0\; \Rightarrow  {P}_{L}^{H} - {P}_{L} = 0 : {P}_{L}^{H} = {P}_{L}$

充分性: 已知 ${P}^{2} = P \Rightarrow  P = {P}_{R\left( P\right) , N\left( P\right) }$

$$
{P}^{\mathrm{H}} = P : N\left( P\right)  = N\left( {P}^{\mathrm{H}}\right)  = {R}^{ \bot  }\left( P\right)
$$

因此 $P = {P}_{R\left( P\right) }$

四、正交投影矩阵的确定方法

$\left. \begin{array}{l} \text{ L的基为 }{\mathrm{X}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{r}} : \;\mathrm{X} = \left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{r}}}\right) \\  {\mathrm{L}}^{ \bot  }\text{ 的基为 }{\mathrm{y}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{n} - \mathrm{r}} : \mathrm{Y} = \left( {{\mathrm{y}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{n} - \mathrm{r}}}\right)  \end{array}\right\}   \Rightarrow  \left\{  \begin{array}{l} {\mathrm{X}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathrm{Y} = \mathrm{O} \\  {\mathrm{Y}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathrm{X} = \mathrm{O} \end{array}\right.$

已求得 ${\mathbf{P}}_{L} = {\mathbf{P}}_{L,{L}^{ \bot  }} = \left( {\mathbf{X} \mid  \mathbf{O}}\right)  \cdot  {\left( \mathbf{X} \mid  \mathbf{Y}\right) }^{-1}$

因为 $\left( {X \mid  Y}\right)  \cdot  \left( {X \mid  Y}\right)  = \left( \begin{array}{l} {X}^{\mathrm{H}} \\  {Y}^{\mathrm{H}} \end{array}\right)  \cdot  \left( {X \mid  Y}\right)  = \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} {X}^{\mathrm{H}}X & O \\  O & {Y}^{\mathrm{H}}Y \end{matrix}\right\rbrack$

所以 ${\left( X \mid  Y\right) }^{-1} = \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} {\left( {X}^{\mathrm{H}}X\right) }^{-1} & 0 \\  0 & {\left( {Y}^{\mathrm{H}}Y\right) }^{-1} \end{matrix}\right\rbrack   \cdot  {\left( X \mid  Y\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} {\left( {X}^{\mathrm{H}}X\right) }^{-1}{X}^{\mathrm{H}} \\  {\left( {Y}^{\mathrm{H}}Y\right) }^{-1}{Y}^{\mathrm{H}} \end{matrix}\right\rbrack$

于是 $\;{\mathbf{P}}_{L} = \left( {\mathbf{X} \mid  \mathbf{O}}\right)  \cdot  \left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} {\left( {\mathbf{X}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathbf{X}\right) }^{-1}{\mathbf{X}}^{\mathrm{H}} \\  {\left( {\mathbf{Y}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathbf{Y}\right) }^{-1}{\mathbf{Y}}^{\mathrm{H}} \end{matrix}\right\rbrack   = \mathbf{X} \cdot  {\left( {\mathbf{X}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathbf{X}\right) }^{-1} \cdot  {\mathbf{X}}^{\mathrm{H}}$

例3: 向量空间 ${\mathrm{R}}^{3}$ 中, $\mathbf{\alpha } = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 1 \\  2 \\  0 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,\mathbf{\beta } = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 0 \\  1 \\  1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,\mathrm{L} = \mathrm{L}\left( {\mathbf{\alpha },\mathbf{\beta }}\right)$ ,求 ${\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}$

解: $X = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 1 & 0 \\  0 & 1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,{X}^{T}X = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} 5 & 2 \\  2 & 2 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,{\left( {X}^{T}X\right) }^{-1} = \frac{1}{6}\left\lbrack  \begin{array}{rr} 2 &  - 2 \\   - 2 & 5 \end{array}\right\rbrack$

$$
{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}} = \mathrm{X}{\left( {\mathrm{X}}^{\mathrm{T}}\mathrm{X}\right) }^{-1}{\mathrm{X}}^{\mathrm{T}} = \frac{1}{6}\left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} 2 & 2 &  - 2 \\  2 & 5 & 1 \\   - 2 & 1 & 5 \end{matrix}\right\rbrack
$$

[注]: 正交投影矩阵 ${\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{L}}$ 与子空间 $\mathrm{L}$ 的基的选择无关

## 广义逆矩阵

## 一、定义与算法

定义:对 ${A}_{m \times  n}$ ，若有 ${X}_{n \times  m}$ 满足Penrose方程

(1)AXA=A (2) $\mathrm{{XAX}} = \mathrm{X}$

(3) ${\left( AX\right) }^{H} = {AX}$ (4) ${\left( XA\right) }^{H} = {XA}$

称 $X$ 为 $A$ 的M-P逆,记作 ${A}^{ + }$ .(Moore 1920, Penrose1955)

例如 ${A}_{n \times  n}$ 可逆, $X = {A}^{-1}$ 满足 $P$ -方程: ${A}^{ + } = {A}^{-1}$

$A = {O}_{m \times  n}, X = {O}_{n \times  m}\;$ 满足 $P$ -方程: ${O}_{m \times  n}^{ + } = {O}_{n \times  m}$

$A = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{l} 1 \\  1 \end{array}\right\rbrack  , X = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right\rbrack  \;$ 满足 $P$ -方程: ${A}^{ + } = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} \\  2 & 2 \end{array}\right\rbrack$

例4: $F \in  {\mathbf{C}}_{r}^{m \times  r}\left( {r \geq  1}\right)  \Rightarrow  {F}^{ + } = {\left( {F}^{\mathrm{H}}F\right) }^{-1}{F}^{\mathrm{H}}$ ,且 ${F}^{ + }F = {I}_{r}$

$$
G \in  {\mathrm{C}}_{r}^{r \times  n}\left( {r \geq  1}\right)  \Rightarrow  {G}^{ + } = {G}^{\mathrm{H}}{\left( G{G}^{\mathrm{H}}\right) }^{-1}\text{ ,且 }G{G}^{ + } = {I}_{r}
$$

验证第一式: 令 ${F}^{ + } = {\left( {F}^{H}F\right) }^{-1}{F}^{H}$ ,则有

$$
\mathrm{{FXF}} = \mathrm{F}{\left( {\mathrm{F}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathrm{F}\right) }^{-1}{\mathrm{\;F}}^{\mathrm{H}}\mathrm{F} = \mathrm{F}
$$

$$
{XFX} = {\left( {F}^{H}F\right) }^{-1}{F}^{H}{FX} = X
$$

$$
{\left( FX\right) }^{H} = {X}^{H}{F}^{H} = F{\left( {F}^{H}F\right) }^{-1}{F}^{H} = {FX}
$$

$$
{\left( XF\right) }^{H} = {I}_{r}^{H} = {I}_{r} = {XF}
$$

定理3: $\forall {\mathrm{A}}_{\mathrm{m} \times  \mathrm{n}},{\mathrm{A}}^{ + }$ 存在并唯一

证明: 存在性 $A = {O}_{m \times  n} \Rightarrow  {A}^{ + } = {O}_{n \times  m}$

$$
A \neq  O \Rightarrow  \operatorname{rank}A \geq  1 : \;A = {FG}, F \in  {\mathbf{C}}_{r}^{m \times  r}, G \in  {\mathbf{C}}_{r}^{r \times  n}
$$

令 $X = {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + }$ 则有

$$
{AXA} = \mathbf{F}\mathbf{G} \cdot  {\mathbf{G}}^{ + }{\mathbf{F}}^{ + } \cdot  \mathbf{F}\mathbf{G} = \mathbf{F}\mathbf{G} = \mathbf{A}
$$

$$
{XAX} = {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + } \cdot  {FG} \cdot  {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + } = {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + } = X
$$

$$
{\left( AX\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {\left( FG \cdot  {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + }\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {\left( F{F}^{ + }\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = F{F}^{ + } = F \cdot  G{G}^{ + } \cdot  {F}^{ + } = {AX}
$$

$$
{\left( XA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {\left( {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + } \cdot  FG\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {\left( {G}^{ + }G\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {G}^{ + }G = {G}^{ + } \cdot  {F}^{ + }F \cdot  G = {XA}
$$

$$
{A}^{ + } = {G}^{ + }{F}^{ + } = {G}^{\mathrm{H}}{\left( {F}^{\mathrm{H}}A{G}^{\mathrm{H}}\right) }^{-1}{F}^{\mathrm{H}}
$$

定理3: $\forall {\mathrm{A}}_{\mathrm{m} \times  \mathrm{n}},{\mathrm{A}}^{ + }$ 存在并唯一

证明: 唯一性,对 ${\mathrm{A}}_{\mathrm{m} \times  \mathrm{n}}$ 若 ${\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{m}}$ 与 ${\mathrm{Y}}_{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{m}}$ 都满足 $\mathrm{P}$ -方程,

则:

$$
X = {XAX} = X \cdot  {AYA} \cdot  X = X \cdot  {\left( AY\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} \cdot  {\left( AX\right) }^{\mathrm{H}}
$$

$$
= X \cdot  {\left( AXAY\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = X \cdot  {\left( AY\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {XAY} = X \cdot  {AYA} \cdot  Y
$$

$$
= {\left( XA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} \cdot  {\left( YA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} \cdot  Y = {\left( YAXA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} \cdot  Y = {\left( YA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} \cdot  Y = {YAY} = Y
$$

则 ${A}^{ + } = V{\left\lbrack  \begin{matrix} \mathop{\sum }\limits_{r}^{{-1}} & O \\  O & O \end{matrix}\right\rbrack  }_{n \times  m}{U}^{H}$

$$

$$
= {\mathbf{U}}_{\mathrm{s}}{\mathbf{\sum }}_{\mathrm{r}}{\mathbf{V}}_{\mathrm{s}}^{\mathrm{H}}
$$

$$
{\mathrm{A}}^{ + } = {\mathrm{V}}_{\mathrm{s}}{\sum }_{\mathrm{r}}^{-1}{\mathrm{U}}_{\mathrm{s}}^{\mathrm{H}}
$$

广义逆矩阵的分类: 对 ${\mathrm{A}}_{\mathrm{m} \times  \mathrm{n}}$ ,若 ${\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{m}}$ 满足 $\mathrm{P}$ -方程

(i): 称 $X$ 为 $A$ 的 $\{ i\}$ -逆,记作 ${A}^{\left( i\right) }$ . 全体记作 $A\{ i\}$

(i),(j): 称 $X$ 为 $A$ 的 $\{ i, j\}$ -逆,记作 ${A}^{\left( i, j\right) }$ . 全体记作 $A\{ i, j\}$

## 二、M-P逆的等价定义

Moore逆: 对 ${A}_{mxn}$ ,若有 ${X}_{nxm}$ 满足 ${AX} = {P}_{R\left( A\right) }$ 和 ${XA} = {P}_{R\left( X\right) }$ ,

称 $X$ 为 $A$ 的Moore逆。

定理11: M-逆与P-逆等价

证明: (1) 设 $X$ 是 $A$ 的M-逆:

$$
{AXA} = {P}_{R\left( A\right) } \cdot  \left( {{a}_{1},\cdots ,{a}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{a}_{1},\cdots ,{a}_{n}}\right)  = A
$$

$$
{XAX} = {P}_{R\left( X\right) } \cdot  \left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{m}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{m}}\right)  = X
$$

$$
{\left( AX\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {P}_{R\left( A\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {P}_{R\left( A\right) } = {AX}
$$

${\left( XA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {P}_{R\left( X\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {P}_{R\left( X\right) } = {XA}\;$ 故 $X$ 是 $\mathrm{A}$ 的 $\mathrm{P}$ -逆

(2) 设 $X$ 是 $A$ 的 $P$ -逆:

$$
\left. \begin{array}{l} {\left( AX\right) }^{2} = {AXAX} = {AX} \\  {\left( AX\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {AX} \end{array}\right\}   \Rightarrow  \mathrm{{AX}} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{{AX}}\right) } = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{A}\right) }
$$

$$
\because \mathrm{A}X\mathrm{A} = \mathrm{A},\;\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{{AXA}}\right)  \subset  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{{AX}}\right)  \subset  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{A}\right)
$$

$$
\left. \begin{array}{l} {\left( XA\right) }^{2} = {XAXA} = {XA} \\  {\left( XA\right) }^{\mathrm{H}} = {XA} \end{array}\right\}   \Rightarrow  {XA} = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{{XA}}\right) } = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{X}\right) }
$$

$$
\because {XAX} = A,\;R\left( {XAX}\right)  \subset  R\left( {XA}\right)  \subset  R\left( X\right)
$$

故 $X$ 是 $A$ 的M-逆
