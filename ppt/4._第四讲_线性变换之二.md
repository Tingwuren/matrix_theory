$$
x = {k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}
$$

从而, $T\left( x\right)  = {k}_{1}T\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}T\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ .

重由此知, $T\left( x\right)$ 由 $T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 完全确定.

设 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 是线性空间 $\mathrm{V}$ 的一组基， ${\mathrm{T}}_{1},{\mathrm{\;T}}_{2}$ 为 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性变换，

若 ${\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{i}}\right)  = {\mathrm{T}}_{2}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{i}}\right) ,\mathrm{i} = 1,2,\cdots ,\mathrm{n}.\;$ 则 ${\mathrm{T}}_{1} = {\mathrm{T}}_{2}$

证: 对 $\forall x \in  V, x = {k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}$

${T}_{1}\left( x\right)  = {k}_{1}{T}_{1}\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}{T}_{1}\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{T}_{1}\left( {x}_{n}\right)$

$$
{T}_{2}\left( x\right)  = {k}_{1}{T}_{2}\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}{T}_{2}\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{T}_{2}\left( {x}_{n}\right)

由已知,即得 ${T}_{1}\left( x\right)  = {T}_{2}\left( x\right) \;\therefore {T}_{1} = {T}_{2}$

重由此知, 一个线性变换完全由它在一组基上的作用所决定.

设 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 是线性空间 $\mathrm{V}$ 的一组基，对 $\mathrm{V}$ 中任意 $\mathrm{n}$ 个向量 ${\mathbf{\alpha }}_{1},{\mathbf{\alpha }}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathbf{\alpha }}_{\mathrm{n}}$ , 都存在线性变换 $T$ 使 $T\left( {x}_{i}\right)  = {\alpha }_{i},\;i = 1,2,\cdots , n$

证: $\forall y \in  V,\;y = {k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}$

定义 $T : V \rightarrow  V, T\left( y\right)  = {k}_{1}{\alpha }_{1} + {k}_{2}{\alpha }_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{\alpha }_{n}$ ,

易知 $\mathrm{T}$ 为 $\mathrm{V}$ 的一个变换,下证它是线性的.

任取 $y, z \in  V$ ,设 $y = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{b}_{i}{x}_{i}, z = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{c}_{i}{x}_{i}$ 则 $y + z = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left( {{b}_{i} + {c}_{i}}\right) {x}_{i},\;{ky} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left( {k{b}_{i}}\right) {x}_{i}$ 于是 $T\left( {y + z}\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left( {{b}_{i} + {c}_{i}}\right) {\alpha }_{i} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{b}_{i}{\alpha }_{i} + \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{c}_{i}{\alpha }_{i} = T\left( y\right)  + T\left( z\right)$

定义 $T : V \rightarrow  V, T\left( y\right)  = {k}_{1}{\alpha }_{1} + {k}_{2}{\alpha }_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{\alpha }_{n}$ , $T\left( {ky}\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\left( {k{b}_{i}}\right) {\alpha }_{i} = k\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{b}_{i}{\alpha }_{i} = {kT}\left( y\right)$

$\therefore \mathrm{T}$ 为 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性变换.

又 ${\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{i}} = 0{\mathrm{X}}_{1} + \cdots  + 0{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{i} - 1} + {\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{i}} + 0{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{i} + 1} + \cdots  + 0{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$

$\therefore \mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{i}}\right)  = {\alpha }_{\mathrm{i}},\;\mathrm{i} = 1,2,\cdots ,\mathrm{n}$

## 1. 线性变换的矩阵

设 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 为数域 $K$ 上线性空间 $V$ 的一组基， $T$ 为 $V$ 的线性变换. 基向量的象可以被基线性表出,设

$$
\left\{  \begin{matrix} T\left( {x}_{1}\right)  = {a}_{11}{x}_{1} + {a}_{21}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {a}_{n1}{x}_{n} \\  T\left( {x}_{2}\right)  = {a}_{12}{x}_{1} + {a}_{22}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {a}_{n2}{x}_{n} \\  \cdots \cdots \\  T\left( {x}_{n}\right)  = {a}_{1n}{x}_{1} + {a}_{2n}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {a}_{nn}{x}_{n} \end{matrix}\right.
$$

用矩阵表示即为 $T\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {T{x}_{1}, T{x}_{2},\cdots , T{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A$ 其中 $A = \left( \begin{array}{llll} {a}_{11} & {a}_{12} & \cdots & {a}_{1n} \\  {a}_{21} & {a}_{22} & \cdots & {a}_{2n} \\   & & & \\  {a}_{n1} & {a}_{n2} & \cdots & {a}_{nn} \end{array}\right)$ 称为线性变换 T 在基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵.

其中 $A = \left( \begin{matrix} {a}_{11} & {a}_{12} & \cdots & {a}_{1n} \\  {a}_{21} & {a}_{22} & \cdots & {a}_{2n} \\  \cdots & \cdots & \cdots & \cdots \\  {a}_{n1} & {a}_{n2} & \cdots & {a}_{nn} \end{matrix}\right)$ ,称为线性变换 $T$ 在基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵.

注:① 给定 ${V}^{n}$ 的基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 和线性变换 $T$ ，矩阵 $A$ 是唯一的.

② 单位变换在任意一组基下的矩阵皆为单位矩阵;

零变换在任意一组基下的矩阵皆为零矩阵;

数乘变换在任意一组基下的矩阵皆为数量矩阵;

例1. 设线性空间 ${\mathrm{V}}^{3}$ 的线性变换 $\mathrm{T}$ 为

$$
T\left( {{\xi }_{1},{\xi }_{2},{\xi }_{3}}\right)  = \left( {{\xi }_{1},{\xi }_{2},{\xi }_{1} + {\xi }_{2}}\right)
$$

求 $T$ 在标准基 ${e}_{1},{e}_{2},{e}_{3}$ 下的矩阵.

解: $\because \mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{e}}_{1}\right)  = \mathrm{T}\left( {1,0,0}\right)  = \left( {1,0,1}\right)$

$$
T\left( {e}_{2}\right)  = T\left( {0,1,0}\right)  = \left( {0,1,1}\right)
$$

$$
T\left( {e}_{3}\right)  = T\left( {0,0,1}\right)  = \left( {0,0,0}\right)
$$

$$
\therefore \mathrm{T}\left( {{\mathrm{e}}_{1},{\mathrm{e}}_{2},{\mathrm{e}}_{3}}\right)  = \left( {{\mathrm{e}}_{1},{\mathrm{e}}_{2},{\mathrm{e}}_{3}}\right) \left( \begin{array}{lll} 1 & 0 & 0 \\  0 & 1 & 0 \\  1 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right)
$$

例2. 设线性空间 ${P}_{n}\left\lbrack  t\right\rbrack$ 的线性变换 $T$ 为 ${Tf}\left( t\right)  = {f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)$

基I为 ${f}_{0} = 1,{f}_{1} = t,{f}_{2} = \frac{{t}^{2}}{2!},\cdots ,{f}_{n} = \frac{{t}^{n}}{n!}$

基II为 ${g}_{0} = 1,{g}_{1} = t,{g}_{2} = {t}^{2},\cdots ,{g}_{n} = {t}^{n}$

记T在基I下的矩阵为 ${\mathrm{A}}_{1}$ ，在基II下的矩阵为 ${\mathrm{A}}_{2}$

$$
{\mathrm{{Tf}}}_{0} = 0,{\mathrm{{Tf}}}_{1} = {\mathrm{f}}_{0},{\mathrm{{Tf}}}_{2} = {\mathrm{f}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{{Tf}}}_{\mathrm{n}} = {\mathrm{f}}_{\mathrm{n} - 1}
$$

$$
{\mathrm{{Tg}}}_{0} = 0,{\mathrm{{Tg}}}_{1} = {\mathrm{g}}_{0},{\mathrm{{Tg}}}_{2} = 2{\mathrm{\;g}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathrm{{Tg}}}_{\mathrm{n}} = {\mathrm{{ng}}}_{\mathrm{n} - 1}
$$

$$
\therefore {A}_{1} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{lllll} 0 & 1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\  0 & 1 & 1 & \cdots & 0 \\   & &  \ddots  &  \ddots  & \vdots \\   & & & 0 & 1 \\   & & & & 0 \end{array}\right\rbrack  ,{A}_{2} = \left\lbrack  \begin{array}{lllll} 0 & 1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\  0 & 0 & 2 & \cdots & 0 \\   & &  \ddots  &  \ddots  & \vdots \\   & & & 0 & n \\   & & & & 0 \end{array}\right\rbrack
$$

线性变换T的值域:

线性变换 $\mathrm{T}$ 的核: $N\left( T\right)  = \{ x \mid  {Tx} = 0, x \in  V\}$

定理: 线性空间 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性变换 $\mathrm{T}$ 的值域和核都是 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性子空间

$$
\mathrm{V} \neq  \varnothing  \Rightarrow  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  \neq  \varnothing
$$

$$
\forall {\mathrm{y}}_{1} \in  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  \Rightarrow  \exists {\mathrm{x}}_{1} \in  \mathrm{V},\text{ st }{\mathrm{y}}_{1} = {\mathrm{{Tx}}}_{1}
$$

$$
\forall {\mathrm{y}}_{2} \in  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  \Rightarrow  \exists {\mathrm{x}}_{2} \in  \mathrm{V},\text{ st }{\mathrm{y}}_{2} = {\mathrm{{Tx}}}_{2}
$$

$$
{\mathrm{y}}_{1} + {\mathrm{y}}_{2} = {\mathrm{{Tx}}}_{1} + {\mathrm{{Tx}}}_{2} = \mathrm{T}\left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1} + {\mathrm{x}}_{2}}\right)  \in  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)
$$

$$
{\mathrm{{ky}}}_{1} = \mathrm{k}\left( {\mathrm{{Tx}}}_{1}\right)  = \mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{{kx}}}_{1}\right)  \in  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)
$$

所以, $\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$ 是 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性子空间

定义:线性变换T的值域 R(T)的维数称为T的秩 T的核 $\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$ 的维数称为T的亏(零度)

例: 在线性空间 ${P}_{n}\left\lbrack  x\right\rbrack$ 中,令

$$
T\left( {f\left( x\right) }\right)  = {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right)
$$

则 $\mathrm{T}\left( {{\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{n}}\left\lbrack  \mathrm{x}\right\rbrack  }\right)  = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{n} - 1}\left\lbrack  \mathrm{x}\right\rbrack$

$$
\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  = \mathrm{P}
$$

所以T的秩为n-1, T的零度为1.

定理: 设 $\mathrm{T}$ 是 $\mathrm{n}$ 维线性空间 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性变换, ${\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 是 $\mathrm{V}$ 的一组基, T在这组基下的矩阵是 $\mathrm{A}$ ,则

1) $\mathrm{T}$ 的值域 $\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$ 是由基象组生成的子空间,即

$$
R\left( T\right)  = L\left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)
$$

2) $T$ 的秩 $= A$ 的秩.

证: 1) $\forall y \in  V$ ,设 $y = {k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}$ , 于是 $T\left( y\right)  = {k}_{1}T\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}T\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}T\left( {x}_{n}\right)  \in  L\left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)$ 即 $R\left( T\right)  \subseteq  L\left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)$ 又对 $\;\nabla {k}_{1}T\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}T\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 有 ${\mathrm{k}}_{1}\mathrm{\;T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{1}\right)  + {\mathrm{k}}_{2}\mathrm{\;T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}}\mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}\right)  = \mathrm{T}\left( {{\mathrm{k}}_{1}{\mathrm{x}}_{1} + {\mathrm{k}}_{2}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} + \ldots  + {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right)  \in  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$

有 ${k}_{1}T\left( {x}_{1}\right)  + {k}_{2}T\left( {x}_{2}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}T\left( {x}_{n}\right)  = T\left( {{k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \ldots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}}\right)  \in  R\left( T\right)$

$\therefore \;\mathrm{L}\left( {\mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{1}\right) ,\mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{2}\right) ,\cdots ,\mathrm{T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}\right) }\right)  \subseteq  \mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$ .

因此, $R\left( T\right)  = L\left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)$ .

2) 由 1), T 的秩等于基象组 $T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 的秩,又

$$
\left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A
$$

$T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 的秩等于矩阵 $A$ 的秩

$\therefore$ 秩(T) $=$ 秩 $\left( \mathrm{A}\right)$ .

设T为n维线性空间V的线性变换, 则

T 的秩 $+ \mathrm{T}$ 的零度 $= \mathrm{n}$

即 $\dim R\left( T\right)  + \dim N\left( T\right)  = n$ .

证明: 设 $\mathrm{T}$ 的零度等于 $\mathrm{r}$ ,在核 $\mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$ 中取一组基

$$
{X}_{1},{X}_{2},\cdots ,{X}_{r}
$$

并把它扩充为 $V$ 的一组基: ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\cdots ,{X}_{r},\cdots ,{X}_{n}$

$R\left( T\right)$ 是由基象组 $T\left( {x}_{1}\right) , T\left( {x}_{2}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 生成的.

但 $T\left( {x}_{i}\right)  = 0,\;i = 1,2,\cdots , r$ .

$\therefore \;R\left( T\right)  = L\left( {T\left( {x}_{r + 1}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)$

下证 $T\left( {x}_{r + 1}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 为 $R\left( T\right)$ 的一组基，即证它们线性无关.

设 ${k}_{r + 1}T\left( {x}_{r + 1}\right)  + \cdots  + {k}_{n}T\left( {x}_{n}\right)  = 0$

则有 $T\left( {{k}_{r + 1}{x}_{r + 1} + \cdots  + {k}_{n}{x}_{n}}\right)  = 0$

$\therefore \mathrm{y} = {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1} + \cdots  + {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}} \in  \mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)$

即 $y$ 可被 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{r}$ 线性表出. 设 $y = {k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \cdots  + {k}_{r}{x}_{r}$ 于是有 ${\mathrm{k}}_{1}{\mathrm{x}}_{1} + {\mathrm{k}}_{2}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} + \cdots  + {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{r}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{r}} - {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1} - \cdots  - {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}} = 0$

于是有 ${k}_{1}{x}_{1} + {k}_{2}{x}_{2} + \; + {k}_{r}{x}_{r} - {k}_{r + 1}{x}_{r + 1} - \; - {k}_{n}{x}_{n} = 0$

由于 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 为 $V$ 的基.

$\therefore {\mathrm{k}}_{1} = {\mathrm{k}}_{2} = \cdots  = {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}} = 0$ 设 ${\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1}\mathrm{\;T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{r} + 1}\right)  + \cdots  + {\mathrm{k}}_{\mathrm{n}}\mathrm{\;T}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}\right)  = 0$

故 $T\left( {x}_{r + 1}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ 线 性无关,即它为 $R\left( T\right)$ 的一组基.

$\therefore \mathrm{T}$ 的秩 $= \mathrm{n} - \mathrm{r}$ .

因此, $\mathrm{T}$ 的秩 $+ \mathrm{T}$ 的零度 $= \mathrm{n}$ .

## 注意:

虽然 $R\left( T\right)$ 与 $N\left( T\right)$ 的维数之和等于 $n$ ,但是 $R\left( T\right)  + N\left( T\right)$ 未必等于 $V$ .

例: 在线性空间 ${P}_{n}\left\lbrack  x\right\rbrack$ 中,令 $T\left( {f\left( x\right) }\right)  = {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right)$

则 $\;R\left( T\right)  = {P}_{n - 1}\left\lbrack  x\right\rbrack  \;N\left( T\right)  = P$

$$
\mathrm{R}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  + \mathrm{N}\left( \mathrm{T}\right)  = {\mathrm{P}}_{\mathrm{n} - 1}\left\lbrack  \mathrm{x}\right\rbrack
$$

定理: 设 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 为数域 $K$ 上线性空间 $V$ 的一组基,在这组基下, $\mathrm{V}$ 的每一个线性变换都与 ${\mathrm{K}}^{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{n}}$ 中的唯一一个矩阵对应,且有以下性质:

① 线性变换的和对应于矩阵的和；

② 线性变换的数量乘积对应于矩阵的数量乘积;

③ 线性变换的乘积对应于矩阵的乘积;

④ 可逆线性变换与可逆矩阵对应, 且逆变换对应于逆矩阵.

证: 设 ${\mathrm{T}}_{1},{\mathrm{T}}_{2}$ 为两个线性变换,在基 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 下的矩阵分别为 $\mathrm{A}\text{ 、 }\mathrm{\;B}$ ,

$$
{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right)  = \left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{A}
$$

$$
{\mathrm{T}}_{2}\left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right)  = \left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{B}
$$

① $\because \left( {{T}_{1} + {T}_{2}}\right) \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)$

$$
= {T}_{1}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  + {T}_{2}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)
$$

$$
= \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A + \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) B
$$

$$
= \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \left( {A + B}\right)
$$

$\therefore {\mathrm{T}}_{1} + {\mathrm{T}}_{2}$ 在基 ${\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 下的矩阵为 $\mathrm{A} + \mathrm{B}$ .

② $\because \left( {{T}_{1}{T}_{2}}\right) \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = {T}_{1}\left( {{T}_{2}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) }\right)$

$$
= {\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{B}}\right)  = {\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{B}
$$

$$
= \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \left( {AB}\right)
$$

$\therefore {\mathrm{T}}_{1}{\mathrm{\;T}}_{2}$ 在基 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 下的矩阵为 $\mathrm{{AB}}$ .

③ $\because \left( {k{T}_{1}}\right) \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {\left( {k{T}_{1}}\right) \left( {x}_{1}\right) ,\cdots ,\left( {k{T}_{1}}\right) \left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right) \; = \left( {\mathrm{k}{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{1}\right) ,\cdots ,\mathrm{k}{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}\right) }\right)  = \mathrm{k}\left( {{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{1}\right) ,\cdots ,{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}\right) }\right)$

$$
= \mathrm{k}{\mathrm{T}}_{1}\left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \; = \mathrm{k}\left( {{\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{A}
$$

$$
= \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \left( {kA}\right)
$$

$\therefore \;\mathrm{k{T}_{1}}$ 在基 $\mathrm{{x}_{1}},\mathrm{{x}_{2}},\cdots ,\mathrm{{x}_{n}}$ 下的矩阵为 $\mathrm{{kA}}$ .

④ 由于单位变换(恒等变换) ${\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{e}}$ 对应于单位矩阵 $\mathrm{E}$ .

所以，

$$
{\mathrm{T}}_{1}{\mathrm{\;T}}_{2} = {\mathrm{T}}_{\mathrm{e}}
$$

与 ${AB} = {BA} = E\;$ 相对应.

因此,可逆线性变换 ${\mathrm{T}}_{1}$ 与可逆矩阵 $\mathrm{A}$ 对应,且逆变换 ${\mathrm{T}}_{1}^{-1}$ 对应于逆矩阵 ${\mathrm{A}}^{-1}$ .

推论: $f\left( t\right)  = {a}_{m}{t}^{m} + {a}_{m - 1}{t}^{m - 1} + \cdots  + {a}_{1}t + {a}_{0}, t \in  K$

T为线性空间 $V$ 的线性变换,且对 $V$ 的基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$

有 $T\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A$

则 $V$ 的线性变换 $f\left( T\right)$ 在基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵是

$$
f\left( A\right)  = {a}_{m}{A}^{m} + {a}_{m - 1}{A}^{m - 1} + \cdots  + {a}_{1}A + {a}_{0}E
$$

定理: 设线性变换 $T$ 在基 ${X}_{1},{X}_{2},\cdots ,{X}_{n}$ 下的矩阵为 $A$ ,

$\mathrm{X} \in  \mathrm{V}$ 在基 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 下的坐标为 ${\left( {\xi }_{1},{\xi }_{2},\cdots ,{\xi }_{\mathrm{n}}\right) }^{\mathrm{T}}$ ,

$T\left( x\right)$ 在基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的坐标为 ${\left( {\mathbf{\eta }}_{1},{\mathbf{\eta }}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathbf{\eta }}_{n}\right) }^{T}$ ,

则有

$$
\left( \begin{matrix} {\eta }_{1} \\  {\eta }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\eta }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)  = A\left( \begin{matrix} {\xi }_{1} \\  {\xi }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\xi }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)
$$

$\begin{array}{l} \text{ 证: 由已知有 } \\  \mathrm{x} = {\xi }_{1}{\mathrm{x}}_{1} + {\xi }_{2}{\mathrm{x}}_{2} + \cdots  + {\xi }_{\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}} = \left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \left( \begin{matrix} {\xi }_{1} \\  {\xi }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\xi }_{\mathrm{n}} \end{matrix}\right)  \end{array}$

$$
T\left( x\right)  = T\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \left( \begin{matrix} {\xi }_{1} \\  {\xi }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\xi }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A\left( \begin{matrix} {\xi }_{1} \\  {\xi }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\xi }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)
$$

又 $T\left( x\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) {\left( {\eta }_{1},{\eta }_{2},\cdots ,{\eta }_{n}\right) }^{T}$

所以

$$
\left( \begin{matrix} {\eta }_{1} \\  {\eta }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\eta }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)  = A\left( \begin{matrix} {\xi }_{1} \\  {\xi }_{2} \\  \vdots \\  {\xi }_{n} \end{matrix}\right)
$$

定理: 设线性空间 $\mathrm{V}$ 的线性变换 $\mathrm{T}$ 在两组基

$$
{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}} \tag{I}
$$

$$
{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n} \tag{II}
$$

下的矩阵分别为A、B, 且从基(I)到基(II)的过渡矩阵是C, 则

$$
\mathrm{B} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AC}}
$$

证: 由已知, 有

$$
T\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) A
$$

$$
T\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right) B
$$

$$
\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) C
$$

于是, $T\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = T\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) C$

$$
= \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) {AC} = \left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right) {C}^{-1}{AC}
$$

由此即得 $\;\mathrm{B} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AC}}$

设A、B为数域K上的两个n阶矩阵, 若存在可逆

矩阵 $\mathrm{P} \in  {\mathrm{K}}^{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{n}}$ ,使得 $\mathrm{B} = {\mathrm{P}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AP}}$

则称矩阵 $A$ 相似于 $B$ ,记为 $A \sim  B$ .

相似是一个等价关系, 即满足如下三条性质:

① 反身性: $\mathrm{A} \sim  \mathrm{A}$ . ( $\because \mathrm{A} = {\mathrm{E}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AE}}$ .)

② 对称性: $\mathrm{A} \sim  \mathrm{B} \Rightarrow  \mathrm{B} \sim  \mathrm{A}$ .

③ (传递性 $= {\mathrm{P\bar{A}}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AB}}$ ; B $\mathrm{A} \in  {\mathrm{Q\bar{S}}}^{-1}\mathrm{{BQ}},\mathrm{Q} = {\mathrm{P}}^{-1}$ .)

$\left( {\because \mathrm{B} = {\mathrm{P}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AP}},\mathrm{C} = {\mathrm{Q}}^{-1}\mathrm{{BQ}}}\right.$

$\Rightarrow  \mathrm{C} = {\mathrm{Q}}^{-1}\mathrm{{BQ}} = {\mathrm{Q}}^{-1}\left( {{\mathrm{P}}^{-1}\mathrm{{AP}}}\right) \mathrm{Q} = {\left( \mathrm{{PQ}}\right) }^{-1}\mathrm{A}\left( \mathrm{{PQ}}\right) \;$

定理: 线性变换在不同基下的矩阵是相似的; 反过来, 如果两个矩阵相似, 那么它们可以看作同一线性变换在两组基下所对应的矩阵.

证: 前一部分显然成立. 下证后一部分.

设 $A \sim  B$ ,且 $A$ 是线性变换 $T$ 在基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵.

$\because B = {C}^{-1}{AC}$ ,令 $\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) C$ 显然, ${\mathrm{y}}_{1},{\mathrm{y}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 也是一组基,且 $\mathrm{T}$ 在这组基下的矩阵就是B.

相似矩阵的运算性质

① 若 ${B}_{1} = {C}^{-1}{A}_{1}C,{B}_{2} = {C}^{-1}{A}_{2}C$ ,则

$$
{\mathrm{B}}_{1} + {\mathrm{B}}_{2} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\left( {{\mathrm{\;A}}_{1} + {\mathrm{A}}_{2}}\right) \mathrm{C},
$$

$$
{\mathrm{B}}_{1}{\mathrm{\;B}}_{2} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\left( {{\mathrm{\;A}}_{1}{\mathrm{\;A}}_{2}}\right) \mathrm{C}.
$$

即, ${A}_{1} + {A}_{2} \sim  {B}_{1} + {B}_{2},{A}_{1}{A}_{2} \sim  {B}_{1}{B}_{2}$ .

② 若 $B = {C}^{-1}{AC}, f\left( x\right)  \in  P\left\lbrack  x\right\rbrack$ ，则

$$
f\left( B\right)  = {C}^{-1}f\left( A\right) C
$$

特别地, $\;{\mathrm{B}}^{\mathrm{m}} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}{\mathrm{\;A}}^{\mathrm{m}}\mathrm{C}$ .

例: 设 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2}$ 为线性空间 $V$ 一组基，线性变换 $T$ 在

## 线性变换给定基下的矩阵计算方法总结

给定线性空间 $V$ 的基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ ,设线性变换 $T$ 在该基下的矩阵为 $A$ 。

## 一、直接法

(1)计算基象组 $T\left( {x}_{1}\right)$ ， $\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ ，并求出 $T\left( {x}_{j}\right)$ 在基下的坐标向量

$$
{\beta }_{1},{\beta }_{2},\cdots ,{\beta }_{\mathrm{n}}
$$

( 2 )写出 $T$ 在给定基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵

$$
A = \left\lbrack  {{\beta }_{1}{\beta }_{2}\cdots {\beta }_{\mathrm{n}}}\right\rbrack
$$

## 二、中介法

(1)选取 V 的简单基，记 ${\varepsilon }_{1},{\varepsilon }_{2},\cdots ,{\varepsilon }_{\mathrm{n}}$ ；

要求 $\mathrm{V}$ 中元素在该基下的坐标能够直接写出

(2)简单基变为给定基的过渡矩阵 $C$ (采用直接法)

(3)计算基象组 $T\left( {\varepsilon }_{1}\right)$ ， $\cdots$ ， $T\left( {\varepsilon }_{n}\right)$ ，并写出在简单基 ${\varepsilon }_{j}$ 下的坐标列向量 ${\mathbf{\beta }}_{1},{\mathbf{\beta }}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathbf{\beta }}_{\mathrm{n}}$ ,以及 $\mathrm{T}$ 在简单基下的矩阵

$$
B = \left\lbrack  {{\beta }_{1}{\beta }_{2}\cdots {\beta }_{\mathrm{n}}}\right\rbrack
$$

(4) 计算 $T$ 在给定基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵

$$
\mathrm{A} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\mathrm{{BC}}
$$

## 三、混合法

(1)选取 V 的简单基，记 ${\varepsilon }_{1},{\varepsilon }_{2},\cdots ,{\varepsilon }_{\mathrm{n}}$ ；

(2)简单基变为给定基的过渡矩阵 $\mathrm{C}$ (采用直接法)

$$
\left( {{X}_{1},\cdots ,{X}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{\varepsilon }_{1},\cdots ,{\varepsilon }_{n}}\right) C
$$

(3)计算基象组 $T\left( {x}_{1}\right)$ , $\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right)$ ，写出在简单基 ${\varepsilon }_{1},\cdots ,{\varepsilon }_{n}$ 下的坐标列向量 ${\beta }_{1},\cdots ,{\beta }_{\mathrm{n}}$ ,以及矩阵 $\mathrm{B} = \left\lbrack  {{\beta }_{1}\cdots {\beta }_{\mathrm{n}}}\right\rbrack$

$$
T\left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {T\left( {x}_{1}\right) ,\cdots , T\left( {x}_{n}\right) }\right)  = \left( {{\mathcal{E}}_{1},\cdots ,{\mathcal{E}}_{n}}\right) B = \left( {{x}_{1},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) {C}^{-1}B
$$

(4) 计算 $T$ 在给定基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 下的矩阵

$$
\mathrm{A} = {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}\mathrm{\;B}
$$
