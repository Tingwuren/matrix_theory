## 过渡矩阵的性质

1、过渡矩阵都是可逆矩阵; 反过来, 任一可逆矩阵都可看成是两组基之间的过渡矩阵.

证明: 若 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n};{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}$ 为 $V$ 的两组基,

且由基 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 到 ${y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}$ 的过渡矩阵为 $C$ ,

即 $\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) C$①

又由基 ${y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}$ 到 ${x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}$ 也有一个过渡矩阵,设为 $B$ ,

即 $\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right) B$②

比较①、②两个等式，有 $\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\cdots ,{y}_{n}}\right) {BC}$

$\left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right)  = \left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) \mathrm{{CB}}$

$\left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\;,{y}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{y}_{1},{y}_{2},\;,{y}_{n}}\right) {BC}\;\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\;,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\;,{x}_{n}}\right) {CB}$

$\because {\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}};{\mathrm{y}}_{1},{\mathrm{y}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ 都是线性无关的,

$\therefore \mathrm{{CB}} = \mathrm{{BC}} = \mathrm{I}$ . 即, $\mathrm{C}$ 是可逆矩阵,且 ${\mathrm{C}}^{-1} = \mathrm{B}$ .

反过来,设 $\mathrm{C} = {\left( {\mathrm{C}}_{\mathrm{{ij}}}\right) }_{\mathrm{n} \times  \mathrm{n}}$ 为 $\mathrm{K}$ 上任一可逆矩阵,

任取 $\mathrm{V}$ 的一组基 ${\mathrm{X}}_{1},{\mathrm{X}}_{2},\cdots ,{\mathrm{X}}_{\mathrm{n}}$ ,

令 ${y}_{j} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{c}_{ij}{x}_{i}, j = 1,2,\cdots , n$

$$
\left( {{\mathrm{x}}_{1},{\mathrm{x}}_{2},\;,{\mathrm{x}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right)  = \left( {{\mathrm{y}}_{1},{\mathrm{y}}_{2},\;,{\mathrm{y}}_{\mathrm{n}}}\right) {\mathrm{C}}^{-1}
